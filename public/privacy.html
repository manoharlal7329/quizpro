Welcome to QuizPro Winner. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide a secure and fair quiz experience:

  • Account Info: Name, Email, and Mobile number for identification.
  • Payment Data: Handled securely by Razorpay. We do not store your full card or bank details.
  • Activity Logs: IP address, device type, and quiz attempts to prevent fraud.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Verifying your identity and preventing multiple accounts.
  • Processing entry fees and prize payouts.
  • Sending critical updates about your sessions.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect data in transit. Access to our database is strictly limited to authorized administrators.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share info with trusted partners like Razorpay for payments and Firebase/OTP providers for authentication.
