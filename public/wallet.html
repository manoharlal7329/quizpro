📊 Why TDS is deducted?

As per Govt. of India rules (Section 194BA), a 30% TDS must be deducted on net winnings from online games. This tax is paid to the government against your PAN card. You can download your TDS certificate every quarter to claim tax credits.

🏆 How to get TDS back?

If your total income is below the taxable limit, you can claim a refund of this TDS by filing your Income Tax Return (ITR) at the end of the financial year.
